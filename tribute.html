Tribute Page
Bill Gates, co-founder of Microsoft Corporation, is a visionary entrepreneur and philanthropist. Born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, Gates demonstrated early aptitude in computing and programming. Alongside Paul Allen, he founded Microsoft in 1975, revolutionizing the personal computer industry with the development of MS-DOS and Windows operating systems. Gates' leadership and innovation propelled Microsoft to global prominence, making him one of the wealthiest individuals in the world. In 2000, he and his wife Melinda established the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, focusing on global health, education, and poverty alleviation. Gates' legacy extends beyond technology, marking him as a pioneer in both business and philanthropy.
